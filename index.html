About Green Haven Project

The Green Haven Project - Mangrove aims to restore coastal ecosystems and raise awareness by planting mangroves and educating participants about the importance of these areas. This six-hour event will involve JCI members, local farmers, students, focusing on combating beach erosion and promoting ESG values
